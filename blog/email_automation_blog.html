From Inbox to Insights: an Automated Email Data Pipeline

An end-to-end pipeline that captures, validates and processes email attachments into actionable business intelligence — making insights available with zero manual intervention.

Type
Automation & ETL
Stack
Power Automate · SharePoint · Power BI
Impact
95% less manual effort

The challenge

Many organisations rely on email to exchange critical business data — monthly sales reports, financial updates, operational logs. The typical workflow has an analyst manually downloading attachments, saving them to a folder, cleaning the data and refreshing a report. It's inefficient, error-prone and creates bottlenecks that delay timely insight.

The challenge was to design a hands-free system that intelligently processes this flow from the moment an email arrives.

The solution: an automated data pipeline

I engineered an end-to-end pipeline on the Microsoft Power Platform. It monitors an inbox for specific emails, validates the attachments, stores them in a centralised SharePoint repository, and automatically triggers a Power BI refresh.

Pipeline architecture

  1. Email ingestion (Power Automate) — a flow monitors an Outlook inbox, triggering only when an email arrives with a specific subject line and attachments.
  2. Smart validation & error handling — attachment names are validated against a naming convention (e.g. Sales_Data_Region_YYYYMM.xlsx); failures alert the sender, otherwise processing continues.
  3. Centralised storage (SharePoint) — validated files are saved to a structured document library, organised by year and month, acting as the data source.
  4. Transformation & refresh (Power BI) — Power BI connects to the SharePoint folder, combines files with Power Query, applies transformations and runs a scheduled refresh so dashboards always reflect the latest data.

The pipeline provides a clear audit trail, tracking data all the way from the inbox to the final report.

Key outcomes & impact

  • Drastic time savings — eliminated several hours of manual processing each month.
  • Real-time decisions — dashboards refresh automatically within hours of data arriving.
  • Scalable & governed — easily extended to new sources, with an auditable lineage throughout.
