From Inbox to Insights: Automated Email Data Pipeline

An end-to-end automated data pipeline that captures, validates, and processes email attachments into actionable business intelligence.

Project Type

Automation & ETL

Role

Data Solutions Architect

Core Technologies

Power Automate, SharePoint, Power BI

Impact

95% Reduction in Manual Effort

The Business Challenge

Many organizations rely on email to exchange critical business data, such as monthly sales reports, financial updates, or operational logs. This manual process is inefficient and prone to human error. A typical workflow involves an analyst manually downloading attachments, saving them to a specific folder, cleaning the data, and refreshing a BI report.

This approach creates significant bottlenecks, delays access to timely insights, and introduces a high risk of data inconsistency. The challenge was to design a hands-free system that could intelligently process this flow of information from the moment an email arrives.

The Solution: An Automated Data Pipeline

I engineered an end-to-end automated data pipeline using the Microsoft Power Platform. The solution monitors an inbox for specific emails, validates the attachments, stores them in a centralised SharePoint repository, and automatically triggers a data refresh in Power BI, making insights available with zero manual intervention.

Pipeline Architecture:

1. Email Ingestion (Power Automate)

A Power Automate flow continuously monitors an Outlook inbox. It's configured to trigger only when an email arrives with a specific subject line (e.g., "Monthly Sales Report") and has attachments.

2. Smart Validation & Error Handling

The flow validates attachment names against a predefined naming convention (e.g., "Sales_Data_Region_YYYYMM.xlsx"). If validation fails, an alert is sent to the sender; otherwise, the process continues.

3. Centralised Storage (SharePoint)

Validated attachments are automatically saved to a structured SharePoint document library, which acts as a data source for Power BI. Files are organized into folders by year and month for clarity.

4. Data Transformation & Refresh (Power BI)

Power BI is connected to the SharePoint folder. Using Power Query, it automatically combines all files, applies data transformation steps, and performs a scheduled refresh of the dataset, ensuring the dashboard always reflects the latest data.

Key Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Drastic Time Savings: Eliminated several hours of manual data processing each month, freeing up analysts to focus on higher-value strategic analysis.
  • Real-Time Decision-Making: Stakeholders no longer have to wait for manual report updates. Dashboards are refreshed automatically within hours of data being sent, enabling more agile and informed decisions.
  • Scalability and Governance: The solution is easily scalable to handle new data sources or email types. It also provides a clear audit trail, tracking data from the inbox to the final report.
