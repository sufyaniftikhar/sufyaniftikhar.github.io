Automated Parent Communication Agent

An intelligent system that monitors student performance and behaviour, automatically drafting personalised emails to parents for timely intervention and praise.

Project Type

AI & Automation

Core Technologies

n8n, LLMs, Excel, Outlook

Sector

Education

Impact

Proactive Communication

The Business Challenge

Teachers spend a significant amount of time manually tracking student progress and initiating communication with parents. This process can be reactive, inconsistent, and time-consuming, especially when managing large classes. Important opportunities for early intervention or positive reinforcement can be missed, and the administrative overhead contributes to teacher workload and burnout.

The Solution: An AI Communication Assistant

I developed an automated workflow using n8n that acts as an AI-powered communication assistant. The solution connects to student data logs in Excel, identifies key events (such as low assessment scores, missed homework, or positive behaviour), and uses a Large Language Model (LLM) to draft context-aware, personalised emails. These are then saved as drafts in the teacher's Outlook account, ready for a quick review and sending.

The Automated Workflow:

1. Scheduled Trigger & Data Ingestion

The workflow runs automatically every weekday afternoon. It connects to Microsoft Excel to read from multiple sheets containing student rosters and academic engagement logs.

2. Data Filtering & Event Routing

The system filters for recent events and checks a communications log to ensure an email hasn't already been sent for a specific event. It then uses a switch to categorise and route each event (e.g., 'Assessment', 'Homework', 'Behaviour').

3. Conditional Logic & Dynamic AI Prompting

For each event, specific rules are applied, such as identifying assessment scores below 60%, missed homework, or positive behaviour notes. Based on the event type and its details, a unique and detailed prompt is constructed to instruct the AI model (GPT-4) to write a supportive and professional email.

4. Email Generation & Logging

The LLM processes the prompt and generates a complete email. The workflow then creates a draft in the teacher's Microsoft Outlook account and appends a record to the communication log in Excel to prevent duplicate messages.

Key Outcomes & Business Impact

  • Proactive & Timely Communication: Ensures parents are informed promptly about both concerns and achievements, fostering a stronger home-school partnership.
  • Reduced Administrative Burden: Frees up teachers from the repetitive task of manually tracking events and writing individual emails, allowing them to focus on teaching and planning.
  • Improved Consistency & Quality: Standardises the tone, format, and quality of parent communications across the school, a professional and supportive voice.
  • Data-Driven Intervention: Leverages real-time data to trigger communications, ensuring that interventions are timely, relevant, and well-documented.
  • Enhanced Teacher Wellbeing: By automating a demanding and time-consuming administrative task, this solution directly contributes to reducing teacher workload and improving job satisfaction.
