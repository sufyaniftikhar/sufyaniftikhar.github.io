NWG Contract Performance Power BI App

An enterprise-level Power BI application designed to centralise and streamline contract management for Northumbrian Water Group's £3 billion AMP7 programme.

Client

Northumbrian Water Group

Role

Lead Data Analyst

Tools Used

Power BI, Power Automate, DAX

Programme Value

£3 Billion

Project Overview

Northumbrian Water Group (NWG) required enhanced visibility into contract performance across their AMP7 (2020-2025) and AMP8 (2025-2030) Asset Management Periods. While their existing Contract Bee system captured comprehensive contract data, it lacked the analytical depth and custom visualisation capabilities needed for strategic decision-making.

As the lead data analyst, I designed and deployed a comprehensive Power BI solution that transformed how NWG's commercial teams monitor contract performance, manage compensation events, and ensure regulatory compliance across their £3 billion infrastructure programme.

The Business Challenge

Contract Bee's standard reporting couldn't provide the custom analytics, interactive filtering, and real-time insights needed by commercial managers, project teams, and compliance officers to effectively oversee 300+ active contracts. This resulted in fragmented, manual reporting processes, a lack of visibility, delayed compliance tracking, and an inability for senior management to get a quick, high-level overview of programme health.

The Solution: A Centralised Power BI App

To address these challenges, I developed and deployed a comprehensive Power BI App. This app served as a central hub, hosting a suite of interconnected dashboards tailored to specific business needs. By connecting directly to the Contract Bee API and other data sources, the app provided a single source of truth for all contract-related data, enabling self-service analytics and eliminating information silos.

Dashboards within the App:

1. Contracts Overview Dashboard

Purpose: To provide project managers with a detailed, real-time view of contract compliance and performance.

Impact: Highlighted overdue actions and compliance metrics, enabling project managers to proactively address issues. This led to a 30% reduction in overdue compensation events and saved over 10 hours per week in manual reporting.

2. Executive Summary Dashboard

Purpose: To offer senior management a high-level, aggregated view of programme performance with time-based analysis capabilities.

Impact: Enabled leadership to quickly assess performance trends (weekly, monthly, quarterly), aiding in strategic decision-making and the optimisation of contract administration.

3. Compliance Deep-Dive Dashboard

Purpose: To allow for granular analysis of compliance data, identifying specific bottlenecks and root causes for delays.

Impact: Provided the evidence needed to address systemic issues, leading to a significant improvement in overall programme compliance and reducing the risk of contractual penalties.

4. Consultant Invoice Dashboard

Purpose: To create visibility over consultant invoices, tracking rates, hours worked, and resource allocation across projects.

Impact: Improved transparency and control over consultancy costs, ensuring accurate financial reporting and more effective resource management.

Development Methodology

Following agile principles, the project was delivered through a structured five-phase approach, ensuring stakeholder engagement and iterative refinement throughout the development lifecycle.

1. Requirements Gathering: Conducted stakeholder workshops with Senior Commercial Managers and Project Managers to define reporting requirements, identify key metrics, and establish success criteria.
2. Data Integration & Modeling: Established Contract Bee API connectivity, designed optimal data models, and created custom DAX measures for performance calculations.
3. Dashboard Development: Built interactive visualisations with advanced filtering, drill-down capabilities, and a responsive design for various user roles.
4. User Testing & Refinement: Conducted iterative testing sessions with end-users, gathered feedback, and implemented optimisations to enhance user experience.
5. Deployment & Training: Deployed the solution, implemented security controls, and delivered comprehensive training to ensure successful adoption.
