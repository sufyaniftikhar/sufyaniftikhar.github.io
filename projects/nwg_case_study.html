NWG Contract Performance Power BI App

An enterprise Power BI application that centralised and streamlined contract management for Northumbrian Water Group's £3 billion AMP7 programme — automating reporting for 300+ contracts and giving commercial teams real-time visibility.

Project overview

Northumbrian Water Group (NWG) needed enhanced visibility into contract performance across their AMP7 (2020–2025) and AMP8 (2025–2030) Asset Management Periods. While their existing Contract Bee system captured comprehensive contract data, it lacked the analytical depth and custom visualisation needed for strategic decision-making.

I designed and deployed a comprehensive Power BI solution that transformed how NWG's commercial teams monitor contract performance, manage compensation events, and ensure regulatory compliance across the £3 billion infrastructure programme.

The business challenge

Contract Bee's standard reporting couldn't provide the custom analytics, interactive filtering and real-time insights needed by commercial managers, project teams and compliance officers overseeing 300+ active contracts. This led to fragmented, manual reporting, limited visibility, delayed compliance tracking, and no quick high-level overview of programme health for senior management.

The solution: a centralised Power BI app

I developed and deployed a Power BI App that served as a central hub hosting a suite of interconnected dashboards. By connecting directly to the Contract Bee API and other sources, the app provided a single source of truth for all contract data — enabling self-service analytics and eliminating information silos.

The solution led to a 30% reduction in overdue compensation events and saved over 10 hours per week in manual reporting.

Dashboards within the app

  • Contracts Overview — a real-time view of contract compliance and performance, surfacing overdue actions so project managers can act proactively.
  • Executive Summary — a high-level, aggregated view with weekly/monthly/quarterly trend analysis to support strategic decisions.
  • Compliance Deep-Dive — granular analysis pinpointing bottlenecks and root causes, reducing the risk of contractual penalties.
  • Consultant Invoicing — visibility over consultant rates, hours and resource allocation, improving control over consultancy costs.

Development methodology

Following agile principles, the project was delivered through a structured, five-phase approach with stakeholder engagement throughout.

  1. Requirements gathering — workshops with senior commercial and project managers to define metrics and success criteria.
  2. Data integration & modelling — Contract Bee API connectivity, optimal data models and custom DAX measures.
  3. Dashboard development — interactive visuals with advanced filtering, drill-down and role-appropriate design.
  4. User testing & refinement — iterative sessions with end users to optimise the experience.
  5. Deployment & training — rollout with security controls and training to drive adoption.
